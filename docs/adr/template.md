## Context

What situation forced this decision? Capture the constraints, the
stakeholders, the alternatives that were on the table, and the
trade-off space. Be specific — future readers need enough background

## Decision

What was decided? State the decision in plain prose, then enumerate
the concrete implementation commitments that follow from it. Be
unambiguous; "we will use X" is better than "we lean toward X."

## Consequences

What are the trade-offs and implications? Cover:

- What this enables that wasn't possible before.
- What this rules out (and why that's acceptable).
- Operational implications (deployment, monitoring, support load).
- Compatibility commitments (e.g., once this contract ships, what
  guarantees are we making?).
- Risks and mitigations.
- Decision-review triggers — under what future conditions should we
  revisit this?
